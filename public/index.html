Teeth step mechanism

Bending die 3

Yellow workpiece before and after bending are shown on the left. Two grey parts fixed on the base turn pink punches toward blue core when green punch support goes down to create final shape of the workpiece. Robot gripper 7

A brown cylinder opens and closes the jaws, permitting them to grasp and release objects. Pink slider, violet coulisse and yellow bar create a tangent mechanism. Blue (or green) slider and two yellow bars create a slider crank mechanism.

Feed way 4

Pink workpieces move along feed way of rectangular section due to the gravity.

Lining up mechanism 1

Yellow workpieces of disk shape on orange rotary table are arranged in line thanks to centrifugal and friction forces. Pink pushing bar will move them one by one to processing position (not shown).

Face gear 10

Reversing mechanism with face gear drive. The pink shaft is input. The blue slider is moved under manual action.
